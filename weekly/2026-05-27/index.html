Why A taps it more · the misclick read

Most of Flow A's extra taps aren't real password recovery. They're misclicks — users hit "forgot password" thinking it's sign-in, then log in fine seconds later. We tracked what forgot-password tappers did next:

1 in 3 Flow A forgot-password taps is a user who knew their password. Email-first removes the confusion: B's misclick rate drops to 1 in 10, and tap counts collapse.

Next on forgot-password: making the login button always tappable, so users stop hitting "forgot password" by mistake. The misclick data above is why we're testing it.

Honest read on completion: the week-1 +14pp gap converged to +2.9pp, login success now tied. Flow B isn't a conversion silver bullet. It's a friction + duplicate win at conversion parity. Still worth shipping.

Critical · raised by Yurii

Verification gap on signup.

An indie barber account can be created today without phone or email being verified. Proposal: gate account creation behind verification.

Today · login
SMS verify · exists
Shipped · stopgap
  • Login gate. Unverified barbers get sent to a verify-phone screen before they can use the app.
  • The account still gets created first, then blocked. Today the error is a generic "wrong credentials" and needs clearer copy.
Proposed · the target
  • Don't create the account until the phone is verified. Nothing to log into until it's real.
  • Everyone verifies on login, phone first (if they have neither, they add one). Works across devices.
  • Runs through Descope once it owns signup (in progress).
